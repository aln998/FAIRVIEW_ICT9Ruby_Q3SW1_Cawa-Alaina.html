Genshin Impact is a game whose world consists of 7 countries, each being ruled by a god or in the game are referred to as "Archons." These 7 archons each possess the power to control the element each of them possess, Pyro, Cryo, Hydro, Electro, Anemo, Geo, and Dendro. They are also able to give "visions" which is the power to be able to control their element as well, this can be given by them to anyone. The story follows 2 traveler siblings, Aether and Lumine who are first seen in the game escaping their destroyed home Khaenri'ah. The Abyss is known to be an organization of beings that come from Khaenri'ah, which was destroyed during the Archon war about 500 years ago. Creatures and monsters from the Abyss are scattered everywhere in Teyvat, but the main villians or handlers of the organization aim to destroy or take revenge of the Archons. This is because of the Archons who destroyed Khaenri'ah, a world or region that was man-mad and was not ruled or governed by an Archon. While traveling through different universes and dimensions, they encountered an unknown god who battled them. This unknown god defeated both of them and took away Lumine, leaving the other sibling Aether behind. Aether was knocked out into a 500 year slumber in the world of Teyvat. He was awaken by a little floating creature Paimon, and now they both travel together across Teyvat seeking to find the powerful Archons for help, in search of his missing sibling Lumine.

!!!LUMINE


Lumine is canonically the sibling who was taken away by the unknown god, for unknown reasons.
